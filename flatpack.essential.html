FLAT PACK «ESSENTIAL»

Meskipun dibuat dengan bahan yang hemat biaya, unit ini dirancang seperti unit portabel flatpack klasik kami yang dibuat di Cina dan oleh karena itu mempertahankan semua fiturnya (sepenuhnya modular, dapat digabungkan, dapat ditumpuk, dikirim dalam paket) yang merupakan "ESENSI" dari paket datar.

Ideal untuk iklim panas dan sedang dan untuk melengkapi rangkaian produk akomodasi Anda dengan unit modular prefab dengan harga yang kompetitif. Speedy House juga dapat menyediakan semua komponen yang diperlukan untuk menyelesaikan struktur komposit (tangga, jalan setapak, dan atap).

SUPPLY OPTIONS (HOW YOU CAN GET OUR FLAT PACKS)

FLAT PACK (COMPLETE)

Complete module, with all its elements (walls inside the Kit).

Advantages: product ready to be marketed quickly (assembly only)

Customers: companies with possibility of assembly, traders with possibility of storage for a sale or rental of the product disassembled or mounted, end user with possibility of assembly (construction companies)

FRAME (COMPLETE)

Already welded, painted, with insulation and all components of the roof and floor pre-installed.

Advantages: normally advantageous import duties, work is limited to wall mounting

Customers: companies with the possibility of buying and installing walls, doors, windows and electrical installations locally

Frame (Pre Welded Steel Components)

Roof frame and floor frame (either painted or not) are already welded.

Advantages: normally advantageous import duties, work is limited to assembly (no need for welding or painting)

Customers: companies / factories with the possibility to paint and install all other locally purchased components

Steel components (Loose)

Ready to be welded, to assemble on site.

Advantages: very competitive transport price, very advantageous import duties (low purchase value), customizable dimensions of the structure

Customers: factories with possibility to weld, paint and install all the other components bought locally
